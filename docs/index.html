    About MSUM ACM

    The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) at Minnesota State University Moorhead is more than just a student organization – we're a community of passionate learners, creators, and innovators.

    Who We Are

    Whether you're just starting your journey in computer science or you're a seasoned programmer, ACM provides the perfect environment to grow your skills, build lasting connections, and contribute to meaningful projects.

    What We Do

    Our chapter focuses on hands-on learning through workshops, coding competitions, collaborative projects, and industry guest speakers. We believe that the best way to learn technology is by doing, sharing, and supporting each other along the way.

    Research Opportunities

    Take your learning beyond the classroom

    Faculty-Led Research

    Collaborate with our distinguished faculty on cutting-edge projects in AI, cybersecurity, software engineering, and data science. Gain hands-on experience and contribute to meaningful research.

    Undergraduate Research Projects

    Develop and lead your own research initiatives. From writing proposals to presenting findings, undergraduate research is one of the most valuable experiences for your academic and professional growth.

    Research Presentations & Publications

    Share your work at conferences, symposiums, and academic journals. Build your portfolio and gain recognition for your contributions to computer science research.
